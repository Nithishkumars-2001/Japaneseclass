About Sakura JP

எங்களை பற்றி — The story behind our spoken Japanese learning platform.

Our Mission

Sakura JP was created to help Tamil speakers learn spoken Japanese in the most natural way possible — by translating real sentences and paragraphs from Tamil into Japanese, with instant feedback on grammar and spelling.
